Good afternoon everyone and welcome to the Crucible Tournament! How does it work? ️ 2 Crucibles go head to head in each bracket ️ All brackets have been picked at random using an online generator ️ There will either be a link to the video that has been entered or an image or video file ️ Take a look at each entry and vote with a ✅ for your favourite entry ️ Remember this is purely based on aesthetics! We’re looking for the best looking Crucible here! ️ You have 24 hours to vote for your favourite in that bracket. Votes will be counted once the 24 hours is up ️ The winner goes on to face their next opponent and the loser graciously leaves the tournament! If you have entered your Crucible into the tournament you have 24 hours to make as much noise as you can about your entry! Shout it from the rooftops and get as many votes as you can! ❌ Don’t forget, if we discover that you are using BOTs to gain votes or we feel there has been foul play, we have the right to disqualify you. May the best (LOOKING) Crucible win! (edited)
